MCM Music Fridays
GROOVE WITH US

The Office for Student Services (OSS) aims to encourage students to take part in the rich and diverse opportunities being offered to them in MCM. Through their active participation in non-academic activities, they are not only able to experience academic progress but also allow themselves to be holistically developed by nurturing their 21st-century skills learning.

For more information on MCM Music Fridays, you may inquire at the MCM Office for Student Services FB Page.
